About Srinivasa Ramanujan Institute of Technology, Anantapur

Srinivasa Ramanujan Institute of Technology (SRIT) is a prestigious institution in Anantapur dedicated to empowering students through quality technical education. With modern infrastructure, active student communities, and a passion for innovation, SRIT hosts a variety of events that celebrate both academic and cultural excellence.

Event Highlights

2023 - TechXpo

Over 800 participants across 20 colleges showcasing 100+ tech projects.

2022 - Cultural Night

A full day of music, drama, and dance with over 30 student performances.

2021 - Sports Carnival

College-wide tournaments in football, cricket, and athletics.
